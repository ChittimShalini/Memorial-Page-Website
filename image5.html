Dr.kalam honors science for community team

On march 8 2011 a man's great pleasure of getting honored with the hands of Former President and BharatRatna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam on 1st March 2011 for the outstanding contribution in the field of science communication. The function was organized jointly by Kalyan -Dombivli Municipal Corporation, Arohan and Dombivli Gymkhana at Savlaram Maharaj Krida Sankul, MIDC, Dombivli. Editor MLA Ravindraji Chavan, MLA Rameshdada Patil, Mayor of KDMC Mrs. Vaijayanti Gujar, Arohan President Mrs. Vinda Bhuskute, Police commissioner K. P. Raghuvanshi, LoksattaKumar Ketkar, IRB President Dattatray Mhaiskar were other dignitaries present for the ceremony.
